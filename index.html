Why Brario?

As a stereotypical child I was always fascinated by technology. Getting my first game console in 1999, the kiwi green gameboy. Being useless on its own, my parents were also nice enough to supply me with my two first games, so I could actually use the thing. The first super mario and pokemon blue. They had some doubts on the pokemon game because of the difficulty, little did they know that in the next few years I was already doing the unlimited rare candy hack.


Fascinated by the idea that you can move a little pixelman along the screen with some buttons formed the foundation of my current interests.
